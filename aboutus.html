KKC's Blog

My name is KKC, I love to Sing, read, play guitar and write.

My name is Oyinkansola Chinyere-Asabe Onwuchekwa, aka KKC. I am a graduate of Geophysics, of the Department of Geosciences, of the prestigious University of Lagos.
I am a multiple award winning artiste, singer, songwriter and guitarist, having won 1) Best Female Artiste In University of Lagos in the Nigerian Meritorious Awards (19th March, 2016) 2) Best Student Female Artiste In the Nigeria Meritorious Awards, all Universities. (10th October, 2016.) 3)) Best Student Artiste In Faculty of Science 2016 Awards (4th November, 2016) 4) Best Student Female Artiste In the West African Awards (20th September, 2017) 5) School of Disciples (RCCG) students award 1st Best Academic Student Of SOD Glory Worship Centre. (17th June, 2018). 6) School of Disciples (RCCG) students award, 2nd Best Academic Student Of SOD Lagos province 4. (17th June, 2018).
I engage in the following sports: swimming, basketball,, badminton, soccer, athletics, javelin, and bicycle riding. I have many gold medals to my credit from various swimming competitions , gold and silver medals from Javelin throwing competitions, and medals from basketball competitions.
I sing, play the guitar and drums. I have recorded over 10 original songs, 7 of which have been released to the general public (All Rounded Teacher, Baby Jo, Smiling at My Phone, Smiling at my phone Remix, Muse, Falling, JONTOLONTO). I also have three music videos to my credit (Baby Jo, Smiling At My Phone and Muse). I have composed over 200 songs, 100 poems and articles. I am working on publishing my first book, which is a book of poems.
I have a 6months work experience as a Geophysical intern at on oil and gas servicing company,in Lagos, Nigeria.
I own and run a Non-Governmental, Non-Profit charity organization, KKC AND THE COMMUNITY (@kkcandthecommunity), which specializes in rendering medical and material help to people and communities in need every year, as often as possible. We have successfully held 5 major outreaches since it was founded in 2015.
I have held a number of leadership positions including the General Secretary of Society of Exploration Geophysicists, University of Lagos Chapter, the Captain of the University of Lagos Swimming Team and the acting Financial Secretary of Geosciences Students Society, University of Lagos, and the First Class representative of my Undergraduate Class in University of Lagos. I am motivated in the pursuit of Women and Youth empowerment, equal rights for all sexes and improving the condition of living for those around me. Working towards my entrepreneurial skills, I run a chain of businesses on the side, an online catering service with my mom called BEAMS KITCHEN and an Online Jewelry Store, BEAD THE WAIST and a skincare product line THE SKINGODMOTHER. I intend to open more business soon and use create awareness about the system of self-employment and develop employment for youths.
